David T. Watkeys

Graduate Student in History | Virginia Tech

Roanoke, VA | davwat22@vt.edu

Graduate student in History at Virginia Tech focused on Indigenous histories, para/post-colonial history, legal systems, and the cultural and environmental narratives of the United States in the twentieth century. Research interests center on the historical construction of law, Indigenous sovereignty, decolonization, war and veterans’ experiences, and public memory. Experienced in archival research, database construction, and scholarly editing, with a commitment to ethical interdisciplinary historical inquiry and public engagement. U.S. Navy veteran with honorable discharge.
